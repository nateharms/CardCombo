CardCombo - What's best for you?

Home | Credit Card Database | Github

Finding the right combination of credit cards to maximize your potential rewards is hard. With hundreds of cards out there and thousands of possible credit card combinations, this make the challenge that much harder. To solve this problem, I made CardCombo, a web app that will help a user find the best combination of credit cards given their reported expenses.

All you have to do is fill in your estimated expenses in the categories specified in the form below and CardCombo can take care of the rest. If you’re looking to add a card to two to your current wallet, feel free to specify the cards you already have in form as well. You can also add your credit score so we make sure to offer you cards that you can get approved for.

If you’re more interested in how cards stack up against each other, check out the Credit Card Database. This is comprised of hundreds of cards with reviews provided by NerdWallet so you can read up on a card before you commit. Or if you're interested in how this tool works, click submit and CardCombo will explain how it works with the results

My hope is that this tool will help you make the most of the cards you have and hopefully help you find ways to make a bit more money.

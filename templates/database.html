The Credit CardDatabase

Below we have our database of hundreds of credit cards! This database was created by using BeautifulSoup to webscrape information from card reviews on NerdWallet. We were able to scrape the rewards rates for 6 common categories: Grocery, Gas, Dining, Hotel, Flights, and everything else. In addition, we were able to determine the required credit needed to get the card, the annual fee, and the value of any annual bonuses. Each entry in the table below correspond to a review by NerdWallet.
